Our Story

From passionate players to connected arenas.

We founded GameCam in Gothenburg after missing the decisive golden point one winter league night. We set out to ensure that never happens again.

2017 • Prototype weekends

Two padel nerds and a broadcast engineer build the first AI tracking rig using open courts after hours.

2019 • Club pilots

GameTraq cameras go live across three indoor venues delivering automated replays for league organisers.

2022 • Introducing Shotgun

We launch the Shotgun ball machine so members can drill with pro accuracy between recorded matches.

Today • Growing the sport

Serving clubs, federations, and event promoters across Europe and the Americas with an integrated padel stack.
