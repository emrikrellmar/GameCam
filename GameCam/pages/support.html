Services

Everything you need to run reliable broadcasts and training.

Installation services

Certified technicians ensure GameTraq optics and Shotgun machines are perfectly positioned and networked.

  • Pre-install surveys with mounting diagrams and cable path plans.
  • Wi-Fi and LTE validation plus redundancy recommendations.
  • Launch day rehearsals so staff are confident before opening night.

Operational monitoring

Remote diagnostics and predictive alerts limit downtime and maintain quality.

  • Continuous sensor checks for lens cleanliness, temperature, and vibration.
  • Shotgun motor health reports and autopause if attention is required.
  • Weekly status summaries with insights for venue management.

Coach enablement

We equip coaches and staff with workflows to turn data into player progress.

  • GameTraq analytics masterclasses focused on padel tactics.
  • Shotgun programming labs for individual, doubles, and junior pathways.
  • Template libraries for reports, sponsor recap decks, and social teasers.
