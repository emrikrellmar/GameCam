Home / Our Story

From a missed golden point to a connected padel ecosystem.

GameCam began as a passion project in Gothenburg. Today we partner with clubs and federations worldwide to make padel more accessible, data-driven, and exciting.

Milestones

Key moments shaping GameCam.

2016 • The idea sparks

A foggy winter night match ends without a replay of the final golden point. Frustrated players decide to design their own automated filming system.

2017 • Prototype courts

Late-night experiments produce the first AI-driven tracking rig mounted above the back glass of a local court.

2019 • GameCam AB is founded

We formalise operations, welcome our first engineers, and deliver beta hardware to three partner clubs.

2020 • Analytics platform

The GameCam cloud launches, providing auto-tagged highlights and tactical dashboards to coaches.

2022 • Shotgun launched

Shotgun, the smart padel ball machine, debuts to bring data-informed drills onto every training court.

2024 • Global expansion

New offices in Madrid and Miami support partner clubs across Europe and the Americas.

What guides us

We build with respect for the sport.

Padel is social, fast, and addictive. Our technology aims to enhance that energy without overshadowing the human connection that makes the sport special.

Serve the community

We reinvest in grassroots events, junior programmes, and coaching initiatives.

Design for inclusivity

Accessibility features and flexible pricing tiers open GameCam to every club size.

Secure by default

We prioritise data privacy and safe sharing for players, parents, and fans.

Always learning

User feedback loops and analytics continually improve our hardware and platform.

Impact by the numbers

12 countries GameCam hardware deployed across Europe and the Americas.
3,800 coaches Certified on GameTraq analytics and Shotgun programming.
4.7 / 5 Average satisfaction rating from members using GameCam-powered clubs.

People first innovation

We bring players, coaches, broadcasters, and venue owners together in the product process. Quarterly advisory panels and beta programs help every update land with immediate value.

From a single court in Sweden to arenas worldwide, our mission stays the same: make every rally count and every training session smarter.
