Match intelligence

AI that understands every stroke and space.

GameTraq fuses wide-angle panoramic capture with neural vision models that detect ball trajectory, player positioning, and point outcomes in real time.

  • Auto framing keeps rallies centred, while smart zoom highlights the action near the net or back glass.
  • Instant tags for smashes, bandejas, volleys, serves, and unforced errors, ready for review minutes after the match.
  • Coach dashboards compare rotations, defensive formations, and shot selection across matches.

Smart capture pipeline

Dual 6K sensors

Stitched to deliver a distortion-free view of both halves of the court.

Edge processing

On-device inference reduces bandwidth while enabling instant highlights.

Adaptive lighting

Dynamic exposure optimised for indoor LEDs, outdoor sun, and mixed environments.

Specs

Purpose-built hardware for every padel venue.

Install & power

  • Slim mount arm compatible with glass, mesh, and truss structures.
  • PoE+ or 230V AC with UPS-ready failover.
  • Weather-rated for outdoor courts down to -15°C.

Connectivity

  • Dual-band Wi-Fi, Gigabit Ethernet, and LTE fallback module.
  • SRT, RTMP, and HLS streaming protocols with AES encryption.
  • Optional API hooks to club apps for scheduling and pay-per-view.

Analytics outputs

  • Shot maps, rally heatmaps, serve placement charts, and player workload metrics.
  • CSV/JSON exports to integrate with third-party analysis tools.
  • Automated highlight reels for social media and member engagement.

A day with GameTraq

  3. Coaches receive ready-to-review playlists and stats in the GameCam dashboard.
