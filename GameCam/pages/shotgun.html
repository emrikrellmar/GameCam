Shotgun — the smarter padel ball machine.

Shotgun launches precise feeds with controllable spin, speed, and trajectory so players can drill like elite teams. Sync it with GameTraq data to target coaching priorities instantly.

Arrange a trial session Explore training programs
Precision control

Recreate match pressure on demand.

Shotgun uses brushless motors and gyroscopic calibration to deliver consistent feeds from the baseline, side glass, or overhead trajectories.

  • Adjust launch height, topspin, backspin, and pace with 1-touch presets.
  • Serve sequences that mirror opponent tendencies captured by GameTraq.
  • Remote control via tablet or smartwatch lets coaches stay on court.

Technical highlights

Ball capacity

Holds 180 padel balls with a carousel feeder for jam-free sessions.

Power & runtime

Removable lithium pack delivers 6+ hours or plug into 230V mains.

Mobility

Lightweight chassis, rugged wheels, and handle for moving between courts.

Programs

Training templates that adapt as players improve.

Baseline builder

Groove consistent rally depth with alternating topspin and slice drives to both corners.

  • Customise cadence from 15 to 26 balls per minute.
  • Randomise placement to simulate tournament pressure.

Net domination

Practice rapid-fire volleys and blocked shots with adjustable reaction windows.

  • Sequence bandeja feeds with optional lob follow-ups.
  • Pair with footwork beacons to reinforce recovery position.

Match replay

Replay real rallies from the last match analysed in GameTraq.

  • Import rally patterns and pace from analytics to mimic opponents.
  • Auto-adjust difficulty as player effectiveness increases.

Connected experience

Shotgun syncs with the GameCam platform so practice and competition flow together.

  • Assign Shotgun sessions directly from match insights dashboards.
  • Track workload, shot success, and fatigue to prevent overtraining.
  • Export drill summaries to share with players or remote coaches.
